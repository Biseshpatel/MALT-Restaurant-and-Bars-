Our Story

Welcome to Malt

Founded in 2018, Malt Restaurant and Bars brings a fresh perspective to Nepali dining. Our concept blends traditional Nepali hospitality with modern culinary techniques, creating a unique experience that honors our heritage while embracing contemporary tastes.

Our chefs source the finest local ingredients to craft dishes that tell the story of Nepal's diverse culinary landscape. From the Himalayan highlands to the Terai plains, each plate is a journey through our country's rich flavors.

Our Mission

To create memorable dining experiences by combining authentic Nepali flavors with innovative presentations, served in a vibrant atmosphere that celebrates both tradition and modernity.
